# Physical
Physical is a digital catalogue of your music on physical media. Build your catalogue by adding the vinyl records, CDs, and cassettes you already own with help from Apple Music to fill in all the details. Plan new additions by adding the music you want and add new finds on the fly by scanning an item's barcode. Enjoy building, organizing, and sharing your collection with the refined, native look and feel of Physical.

![Physical – Features](https://github.com/spencerhartland/Physical-iOS/blob/main/Screenshots/Physical-Features.png)

## Technologies and frameworks
- SwiftData
- MusicKit
- SwiftUI
- UIKit

### PhysicalMediaKit
[PhysicalMediaKit](https://github.com/spencerhartland/PhysicalMediaKit) is a SwiftUI framework providing easy-to-use, customizable, animated 3D models of physical media.
